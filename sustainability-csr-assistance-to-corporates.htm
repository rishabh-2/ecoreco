Ecoreco enables corporates’ meeting their Environmental, Social and Corporate Governance (ESG) mandates. Ecoreco’s every single activity is a CSR in itself be it recycling of hazardous end of life electronic equipment, training of Kabadies, business opportunity to Kabadies by integrating in formal recycling system, donating refurbished systems to improve computer literacy etc. Inclusive growth and sustainability are at the core of our strategy and approach to Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).

 

We work with corporates to strategically build e-waste management into their CSR programs and utilise their resources for the multiple benefits.

 
Community & Social Development
 
A Greener Future for the Blind

Ecoreco, in association with Times Environ (An initiative of Times of India Group) has pledged to donate computers to the Mumbai branch of the National Association for Blind. Funds generated from the e-waste donations of large enterprises have been used for creating Computer Lab for the NAB besides this Ecoreco has done similar such programs for Cancer Patients Aid Association, Handicap Organisation, Schools & other educational institutes.

 
Every drop makes a difference

For every kilogram of e-waste disposed through Ecoreco, we donate:

 
 1
 
if disposed by us
 
 5
if collected from your premises free of cost
 10
if delivered at our facility free of cost
 
 

Contributions accrued above the value of Rs. 100,000 can be utilized as per the client’s suggestion. Contributions are made through a trust working for the upliftment of the under privileged.
